Extra Classes Resources

We provide additional resources and materials to supplement learning in our extra classes. Below are some of the resources available:

  • Lecture notes: Detailed notes from each class session to review key concepts.
  • Assignments: Practice assignments to reinforce learning and assess understanding.
  • Practice exercises: Additional exercises to practice and improve skills.
  • Recommended reading materials: Books, articles, and online resources recommended by instructors for further study.

These resources are designed to enhance your learning experience and support your academic growth.
